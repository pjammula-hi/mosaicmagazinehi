Celebrating Student Voices

Mosaic Magazine is the proud student publication of Home Instruction Schools. We are dedicated to showcasing the incredible creativity, resilience, and diverse voices of our student body. Through writing, art, and projects, Mosaic serves as a platform for expression and connection.

Our mission is to celebrate every student's unique journey and contribution to our community.
