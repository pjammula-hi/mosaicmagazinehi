# Mosaic Magazine HI - Platform Overview
**A Modern K-12 Digital Publishing Platform**


## üéØ What is Mosaic Magazine HI?

Mosaic Magazine HI is a comprehensive online magazine platform designed specifically for K-12 students and teachers in Home Instruction Shools, a D75 School in NYC. It empowers students to publish their creative work‚Äîstories, poetry, artwork, photography, and more‚Äîin a professional digital magazine format that celebrates student voice and creativity.

Think of it as a combination of:
- **A student newspaper** (but digital and more engaging)
- **A creative portfolio platform** (where students showcase their best work)
- **A collaborative publishing studio** (where teachers and editors work together to publish student content)

1. User logs in with their email
